At the age of six, my father gifted me my first guitar and it has been my main hobby ever since. For almost two years now I have been teaching guitar lessons to students from all ages. Whether it's the first time you pick a guitar, or you have a performance next week, my passion is to build your passion for guitar.

Lately I have been interested in the world of entrepreneurship. Wanting to start young, I called a couple friends of mine and we started a house sanitation business during the Covid-19 pandemic. After a couple months we have a fully functioning business and have grown to handful of employees. I plan on being involved in the entrepreneurial clubs at UTD, and am aspired to grow my experience and knowledge with business.
